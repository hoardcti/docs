Enterprise level **cyber threat intelligence**, designed to run autonomously, seperated by modules for ease of maintenance, and open-source contribution.

## Philosophy

Hoard CTI was create to give everyone access to **"enterprise-level" threat intelligence**, allowing anybody to build apon Hoard CTI's published intelligence free-of-charge.

### Autonomous Approach

Traditional, cyber threat intelligence relies heavily on manual processes to gather, analyse, and contextualise threat intelligence.

However, Hoard CTI introduce a automation-first approach powered by serverless actions, automation policies, minimal AI-workflows, 
with a perfect mixture of aggregation, relations, standardisation, security policies, and open-source contributions.

You can use the data Hoard CTI free-of-charge with little to no limits, And it is all ran autonomously.

### Modularised

Hoard CTI is broken up into diffrent repositories each repo is its own threat intel feed.

When all repositories are combined you are left with a fully featured cyber threat intelligence feed that is well and easily maintained.
You can also help Hoard CTI to be more useful by contributing!
